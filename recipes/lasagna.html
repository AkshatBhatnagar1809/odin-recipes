World's Best Lasagna!


Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna in the order given below:

    Layering:

    1. Meat Sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta mixture
    4. Mozzarella slices
    5. Meat sauce
    6. Parmesan cheese
    7. Repeat the layers, then top with remaining Parmesan

  5. Cook for 50 minutes in 375 degrees F preheated oven. Cover with foil for first 25 minutes then let it cook uncovered for the rest
  6. Let the lasagna rest for 15 minutes at room temperature before serving
