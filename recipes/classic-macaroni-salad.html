Garlic Herbed Chicken


This creamy macaroni salad always gets lots of compliments. It's an easy recipe to make with macaroni pasta, celery, onion, bell pepper, carrot, and pimentos and has a pleasing tangy dressing that everyone seems to love!

Ingredients:

  • Macaroni
  • Condiments - Mayonnaise and Yellow Mustard
  • Sugar and White Vinegar - to taste
  • Seasonings - Salt and Ground Black Pepper
  • Vegetables- Celery, Onion, Bell Pepper, Grated Carrots, Pimento Peppers
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Steps:

  1. Cook the macaroni in salted water.
  2. Mix together Mustard, Mayonnaise, seasonings, vinegar and sugar to taste.
  3. Stir in the macaroni and remaining ingredients. Chill in refrigerator
